Our Story

The story behind Startup Niger Delta

Founded in 2020, Startup Niger Delta emerged from a vision to transform the entrepreneurial landscape of the Niger Delta region. We recognized the immense potential of local innovators and the need for a supportive ecosystem that could nurture their ideas into successful businesses.

Our mission

Empowering entrepreneurs to build sustainable businesses in the Niger Delta

We are committed to creating a thriving startup ecosystem in the Niger Delta by providing access to funding, mentorship, training, and networking opportunities. Our goal is to unlock the potential of local talent and drive economic growth through innovation.
