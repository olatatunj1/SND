FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of startups do you support?

We support early-stage and growth-stage startups across various sectors including technology, agriculture, healthcare, and renewable energy in the Niger Delta region.

How can I apply for funding?

You can apply for funding through our online application portal. Simply submit your business plan, financial projections, and team information for review.

What mentorship opportunities are available?

We offer one-on-one mentorship with experienced entrepreneurs, group workshops, and access to our network of industry experts and advisors.

Do you provide training programs?

Yes, we offer comprehensive training programs covering business development, financial management, marketing, and technical skills development.

How do I join your network?

Join our network by registering on our platform, attending our events, or connecting with us through our various channels.

What regions do you serve?

We primarily serve the Niger Delta region, working with startups and entrepreneurs across all states in the area.

Driving empowerment, training, connecting, and funding startups in the Niger Delta to build sustainable businesses and create economic opportunities across our communities.
